Our Mission

The Applied Network Intelligence Lab (ANIL) is dedicated to advancing the frontiers of network science by developing and applying state-of-the-art AI and machine learning algorithms. Our work focuses on understanding complex systems, modeling dynamic interactions, and creating intelligent solutions for impactful domains such as social networks, biological systems, and infrastructure resilience.

Core Research Areas

Graph Neural Networks

Developing novel GNN architectures for representation learning, link prediction, and node classification on large-scale graphs.

Temporal & Dynamic Networks

Modeling the evolution of networks over time to understand and predict dynamic processes and information diffusion.

Network Robustness & Security

Analyzing the vulnerability of networks to failures and attacks, and designing algorithms for more resilient systems.
